Great design happens when everyone cares

DesignContest is the crowdsourcing website. Here you can start a contest and have something designed by many professional graphic designers according to your brief.

Authorization

There are two types of users in the system

I’m a customer:

the person who needs something designed and is called a Contest Holder

I’m a designer:

the person who can deliver the design

Start a contest

More than 50 design categories for you to launch contest in

It takes only 4 steps to get easy start

Contests list

After you launch a contest in gets to the contests list: all designers can see it and decide whether they want to work with you or not

Contest page

Contest page has 5 tabs, 4 states and various views: clients, designers, administrators and unregistered users see it differently

VOTING

Our voting feature allows the contest holder to add other people to the process of choosing the winner

You can embed the voting code into your site and ask your friends, colleagues or regular visitors to vote for the design they like the most

Congratulations to the Winnner!

After the contest reaches its deadline it’s time for the contest holder to select the best design and start finalization process

Designers Public Portfolios

Designers can create their own portfolios with the help of DesignContest

Connect your behance

The integrated option allows designers to import their Behance artworks to DesignContests portfolio just in a few clicks

Customizable tabs are allowed

iOs application

We are currently developing an iOS application for designcontest.com that will allow you to work with the service even faster and easier.
